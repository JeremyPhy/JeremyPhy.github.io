The PEGASIS (Primary Extracting Analyzing Soil Sampler) is a robotic arm capable of retrieving and analyzing soil samples for mass, temperature, volume and colorimetry.

This prototype was designed for remote operation on a mars rover and emulates the cutting edge technology. It has a precise autonomous control system with a live video feed, 13 integrated elements and 4 degrees of freedom.
