My manifesto



If I had to use one sentence to summarize how I live, it would be: Do what you can't.

It's my passion to take on new challenges and conquer them and it manifests itself in my work and leisure. I knew nothing about front end software, but I learned and made the website you're reading right now. I could barely balance on a skateboard, but I learned and now regularly use one to get around on campus. I didn't know how to run an organization, but I learned and led MGCI robotics to a championship-finish at the UOIT Engineering Sumobot Competition.

At the start, I'm completely out of my element — a fish out of water — but by the end, I wonder why I didn't start sooner. So, whether it be: learning how to skateboard in the middle of winter or building a website from scratch, I'm always pushing my limits.

In my free time, you can find me skateboarding, watching great movies and cheering on the Raptors.
