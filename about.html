My manifesto



If I had to summarize how I live in one sentence it would be: Do what you can't.

Taking on new challenges and conquering them is my passion and it manifests itself in my work and leisure. I knew nothing about front end software, but I learned. I could barely balance on a longboard, but I learned. I didn't know how to lead an organization, but I learned.

At the start, I'm completely out of my element — a fish out of water — but by the end, I wonder why I didn't start sooner. So, whether it be: learning how to skateboard in the middle of winter or building a website from scratch, I'm always pushing my limits.

In my free time, you can find me skateboarding, watching great movies and cheering on the Raptors.
